About OPEN At Wits

OPEN at Wits provides an ecosystem for digital technology and innovation.

OPEN at Wits is located in the new Tshimologong Precinct, initiated by the Johannesburg Centre for Software Engineering of Wits University. It is situated two blocks south of Senate House, and in the middle of Braamfontein.

Tshimologong will bring together local and international, young and experienced professionals, academics, corporates to play an active role in facilitating an environment that fosters innovation and entrepreneurship.

OPEN at Wits is perfectly located to help develop and grow the confluence between software, hardware and content.
