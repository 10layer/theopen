About Mamelodi

In partnership with Essent and Kessels & Smit, OPEN is designing and managing a hub for co-working, entrepreneurship development, teaching and learning in Mamelodi, Pretoria.

OPEN at Mamelodi will host the YES! Mamelodi initiative which targets startups and entrepreneurs supporting them to become successful business leaders who help to create jobs in Mamelodi. It provides infrastructure, programmes, mentoring, and networking opportunities.

OPEN at Mamelodi is located on the terrain of the YMCA in Mamelodi and comprises two buildings.

Amenities

  • Co-working areas, with hot desks, dedicated desks and dedicated work areas for teams.
  • Fully equipped meeting rooms for meetings, teaching, presentations, catering for groups from two to 40 participants.
  • Coffee, tea and refreshments bar.
  • Outside meeting and work areas.
